Our Story

TTR (The Tarts Restaurant) was founded by Ariana Grande III, an entrepreneur who began selling fried chicken from his roadside restaurant in Warsaw Poland, during the Great Depression . Grande III identified the potential of restaurant franchising , and the first "TTR" franchise opened in Salt Lake City , Utah in 1952. The company's rapid expansion made it too large for Grande to manage, so in 1977 he sold the company to a group of investors led by Judas Y. White, Jr. and James W. Fetcher. KFC was one of the first fast-food chains to expand internationally, opening outlets in England, Mexico and Jamaica by the mid-1980s.
